Our Story

35 Years of Pure Flavour & Tradition

It all began in a small lane in Dadar, Gonda, where our founder Shri Rameshbhai Sharma started serving his mother's secret Dahi Bada recipe to neighbours and passersby. What started as a humble street stall has grown into Gonda's most beloved vegetarian restaurant.

We believe that food made with honest ingredients and genuine care tastes unlike anything else. Every Dahi Bada we serve is prepared from scratch each morning — the lentils soaked overnight, the yogurt hung and whisked fresh, the chutneys ground by hand.

Three decades later, our family still cooks with the same values: no shortcuts, no compromises, and a deep respect for India's vegetarian culinary heritage.

Our Signature

The Legendary Dahi Bada That Started It All

Soft lentil dumplings, soaked to perfection, draped in thick chilled yogurt, and crowned with date-tamarind chutney, fresh mint chutney, roasted cumin, and a pinch of secret spice. This is not just a dish — it is a memory in every bite.

Available in three versions: Classic, Special (extra toppings & pomegranate), and Premium (with dry fruit garnish). All made to order.

Meet Our Chefs

Each chef at Dahi Bada brings decades of experience and a personal passion for authentic Indian vegetarian cooking.

Rameshbhai Sharma
Founder & Head Chef

The visionary behind Dahi Bada, Rameshbhai has been perfecting his mother's recipe for over 40 years. His mantra: "Only the best ingredients, only the freshest produce."

📸 👤
Sunita Joshi
Sweets & Desserts Chef

Sunita trained in Mathura and Vrindavan before joining us. Her Gulab Jamun and Kaju Katli have earned their own loyal following of customers who visit just for her creations.

📸 👤
Mohanlal Verma
Chaat & Snacks Specialist

25 years on the streets of Old Delhi before moving to Gonda. Mohanlal brings the authentic flavours of Chandni Chowk to every plate of Bhel Puri and Raj Kachori he crafts.

📸 👤
Priya Sharma
Thali & Regional Cuisine

Daughter of the founder, Priya studied at IHM Gonda and specialises in regional Indian vegetarian thalis. Her Rajasthani Thali is the restaurant's fastest-growing favourite.
